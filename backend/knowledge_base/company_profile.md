# 4-Tier Engagement Model
- Tier 1: Immediate Advisory Support / Advisory & Diagnostics (On-Demand / No Retainer) — targeted diagnostics, audits, KPI or workflow reviews, executive advisory, PMO maturity assessments, project and vendor diagnostics
- Tier 2: Project Recovery & Operational Remediation (Fixed-Scope) — stalled/failing initiative recovery, governance framework installation, execution reset and reporting
- Tier 3: Governance & PMO Retainer / Fractional PMO (Ongoing Partnership) — fractional PMO oversight, ongoing oversight of key initiatives, executive reporting and accountability, risk management and escalation
- Tier 4: Enterprise Excellence / Managed PMO (Embedded Execution) — fully embedded execution leadership, end-to-end governance and delivery, scalable execution systems

# Execution Model
- Senior-led engagement model
- Governance-first execution approach
- Capability transfer to internal teams
- Long-term value over short-term intervention

How FaithForge works: assess execution risk → install governance and structure → enable teams with clarity and tools → transfer ownership and capability.
