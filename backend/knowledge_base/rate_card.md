# Service Tiers (Monthly Packages)

FaithForge packages engagements into three monthly service tiers. Match the tier to the client's size and complexity.

## FORGE ESSENTIAL — Starting at $9,000/month
Ideal for small businesses and growing organizations needing structure, visibility, and light PMO support.
- **Included roles:** PMO Coordinator, Business Analyst, Administrative Support, OCM Specialist (light support)
- **Key deliverables:** Weekly status reporting, RAID management, meeting coordination, basic process documentation, executive monthly summary
- **Support:** Up to 30 support hours/month

## FORGE PROFESSIONAL — Starting at $20,000/month
Ideal for mid-sized organizations managing multiple initiatives or undergoing transformation.
- **Included roles:** Senior Consultant (oversight), Project Manager, Business Analyst, PMO Coordinator, OCM Specialist, Solution Developer (shared hours), Administrative Support
- **Key deliverables:** Portfolio governance, KPI dashboards & reporting, change management support, process optimization, weekly stakeholder governance, automation enhancements
- **Support:** Up to 80 support hours/month

## FORGE ENTERPRISE — Starting at $40,000/month
Ideal for enterprise, regulated, and government clients with complex delivery environments.
- **Included roles:** Program Director, Principal Consultant, Senior Consultant, Project Manager, Business Analyst, PMO Coordinator, Solution / Technical Architect, Solution Developer, OCM Lead, Administrative Support
- **Key deliverables:** Enterprise PMO leadership, strategic roadmap & oversight, compliance & audit readiness, vendor management, executive steering committee support, transformation execution
- **Support:** Up to 160 support hours/month
