## Project Rescue & Turnaround Services — "Recover. Stabilize. Deliver."

A structured intervention for projects, programs, or transformation initiatives that are underperforming, stalled, or at risk of failure. FaithForge doesn't just assess — it partners with organizations to resolve the problem.

### Who typically needs this
Enterprise system implementations, ERP transformations, PMO-led strategic initiatives, compliance remediation, software development programs, cloud migrations, integration programs, vendor-led implementations, digital transformation, government-funded initiatives — especially organizations lacking internal PMO maturity, or juggling multiple vendors/stakeholders on a compliance-heavy program.

### Real warning signs a prospect may be living with (use these for specific, credible observations — never invent which one applies to a given lead unless their context says so)
- Delivery: repeated missed milestones, schedule slippage, unclear priorities, constant rework
- Financial: budget overruns, burn rate exceeding forecast, poor cost visibility
- Governance: no clear decision-making authority, unresolved escalations, weak executive oversight, poor status reporting
- Resourcing: team burnout, skill gaps, key-person dependency, vendor underperformance
- Business alignment: stakeholders disagree on scope, shifting requirements, weak user adoption planning
- Technical: SAP/ServiceNow/cloud & API integration failures, data migration failures, security or compliance blockers

### The 5-phase methodology (useful for a credible one-line description, never list all 5 in an email)
1. Rapid Assessment & Discovery (wk 1-2) — root cause analysis, risk heatmap
2. Stabilization (wk 3-4) — freeze noncritical work, escalate blockers, establish command structure
3. Recovery Strategy & Replanning (wk 5-6) — rebaseline schedule/cost, realign resources
4. Governance Reset (wk 7-8) — steering committee, executive dashboards, change control
5. Execution Support & Delivery Assurance (ongoing) — FaithForge can lead recovery execution through go-live and hypercare

### Engagement models (real entry offers — use the name and duration, never invent a price in an email)
- **Assessment Only** — independent diagnostic, 2-4 weeks, starting from $13,000+
- **Rescue Sprint** — hands-on recovery engagement, 2-8 weeks, starting from $22,000+
- **Embedded Rescue Leadership** — full-time or fractional Program Director / PMO Lead / Project Manager / Recovery Lead, 1-12 months, custom pricing

### Voice notes specific to Project Rescue outreach
A rescue-angle email should sound like someone who has actually walked into a burning program before — not a generic "PMO diagnostic" pitch. Reference a REAL warning sign from the list above only when it plausibly fits the lead's role/segment/context; otherwise keep the observation about them general and let the FaithForge angle stay about steady delivery execution rather than forcing a "rescue" framing onto someone with no signal of a troubled project.
